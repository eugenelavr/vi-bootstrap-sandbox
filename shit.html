vi collects first and third party data and builds unique user segments. Machine learning increases campaign efficiency and optimally matches marketing goals to user interests.

vi is one of the 10 fastest growing companies in Europe (Inc. 5000 in 2016). We are headquartered in Switzerland and have six international offices.

What’s in it for Marketers?

Predictive Mobile Marketing

Mobile is the only growing media consumption channel. What’s more, mobile is the most personal and immediate route to consumers. vi´s predictive technology enables marketers to drive cost-effective results from this unique opportunity.

Performance Marketers: Achieve Your Unique Objectives

vi anticipates a user’s unique intent, allowing marketers to track engagement, purchase actions and total customer lifetime value. We can also apply our data to optimize your video creative at no additional charge.

Brand Marketers:
Reach Your Ideal Audience

Choose from 170+ user segments and let vi’s intelligent technology handle the rest. Your next campaign will only be seen by consumers for whom your message will truly resonate. This means we guarantee 100% fraud free delivery.

What’s in it for Mobile App Publishers?

Customized Integration of Video Player

You can customize both functionality and the look and feel of all video ads shown natively in your app. Whether you choose to use our SDK or API, you’re in complete control of every user interaction.

High Acceptance & Engagement

Mobile users demand non-intrusive experiences that speak to their unique interests. We enable you to successfully deliver highly-targeted ad experiences, so that you have happier, more engaged users who keep coming back.

Highest eCPM with Premium Video Ads

We hold auctions between premium marketers wanting to reach target users through your app. Thanks to our user profiling, you can now deliver 100% validated audiences, in return you get the highest eCPMs in the market.
